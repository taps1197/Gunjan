Gunjan

AUUM Platform initailve

OVERVIEW
Gunjan is a web-app for the college going peeps. For both the students and the admin, its a solution to skip long cumbersome leave forms!
CHALLENGE
Who likes to fill up long forms by pen and paper?
No one! There must be a better way.
This was what a bunch of college students were going through in one of the premier institutes of India. The students had to fill up leave forms in order to take permission to leave the college premises. If this was not enough they had to manually fill their details and timings while leaving or entering the college. The college admins never gave a thought to deal with this nor they had any way of tracking students entering or leaving the premisies. This was a security blunder
SOLUTION
The aim was to run this whole system online. 
As I discussed with students about their problems, I realized a clean and minimal approach was necessary. Two key features were important: portability and accessibility.
While students were the key part, the admins also played a major role. The admins wanted an easy way to manage forms and track no of students outside the premises.
